Manage authors for coverpage PDF
================================

The PDF coverpage contains some optional space for the authors of the 
document.

.. image:: images/coverpage.png
   :scale: 50 % 

First visit the content folder of the related conversion folder

.. image:: images/edit-authors-2.png

and then click on its ``Edit`` tab.

.. image:: images/edit-authors-3.png

A list of author names can be entered (one per line) into the ``Creators``
text field.

.. image:: images/edit-authors-4.png
